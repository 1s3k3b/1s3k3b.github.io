i!emojify

Category: Text

Replace letters and optionally numbers with emojis in a text.

Examples

i!emojify test123 --num

i!emojify aábcdeé --filter

i!emojify testing

Args

<text>: The text to emojify.

Flags

--num, --number: Whether to replace numbers in the text.

--filter: Whether to filter out non-emojified characters in the text.
