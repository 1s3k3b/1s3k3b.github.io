i!booru

Category: Weeb

Get booru images.

NSFW

Examples

i!booru

Aliases

danbooru

Args

[site]: The site to use: `danbooru.donmai.us`, `danbooru`, `konachan.com`, `konachan`, `konachan.net`, `konachannet`, `yande.re`, `yandere`, `lolibooru.moe`, `loli`, `lolibooru`, `gelbooru.com`, `gelbooru`, `rule34.xxx`, `r34`, `rule34`, `safebooru.org`, `safebooru`, `tbib.org`, `tbib`, `xbooru.com`, `xbooru`

Flags

--noembed, --noem: Whether to omit embeds from messages.

--tags: A list of tags to search for, seperated by commas.

--exclude: A list of tags to exclude, seperated by commas.
