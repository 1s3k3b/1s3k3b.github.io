Weeb Commands

Weeb, and weeb NSFW commands.

Commands

i!animeme

Get a random meme from r/animemes.

i!bodypillow

Search body pillows on dakimakura.com

i!booru

Get booru images.

i!doujin

Get info and images of a doujin on nhentai.net

i!hentai

Naked cartoons.

i!japanese

Conversions between hiragana, katakana and romaji.

i!jisho

A japanese dictionary via jisho.org

i!thiswaifudoesnotexist

Get an image of an AI-generated waifu. (thiswaifudoesnotexist.net)

i!waifu

Get info about, or search for waifus.

i!weebify

Weebify a given text.

i!whatanime

Find what anime a character is from based on an image. [(credit)](https://trace.moe)
