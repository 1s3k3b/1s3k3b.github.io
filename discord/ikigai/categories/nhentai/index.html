Nhentai Commands

Commands to interact with nhentai.net.

Commands

i!nhentai sauce

Get info and images of a specified or random doujinshi.

i!nhentai search

Search doujinshi.

i!nhentai homepage

Get doujinshi listed on the main page.

i!nhentai tag

Find a tag or get a list of tags.

i!nhentai artist

Find an artist or get a list of artists.

i!nhentai character

Find a character or get a list of characters.

i!nhentai parody

Find a parody or get a list of parodies.

i!nhentai group

Find a group or get a list of groups.
